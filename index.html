HOX Design Co. is the creative moniker of dynamic Erie, PA-based graphic designer and artist Jordan Sigmund.

Raised in an arts-oriented household, Jordan devoted much of his youth to music and touring the United States in his band. He began designing and illustrating out of necessity for that project, as well as to help his peers locally and nationally. His work as the in-house designer for The Andromeda Agency helped him develop an intuition for work aimed at the independent music industry, while simultaneously developing skills in handling and designing for a variety of print mediums working for a local t-shirt shop.

Through self-instruction and inquisitive experimentation, he developed a style inspired in equal parts by nostalgic Americana, surrealism and analog formats. His appreciation for all things tactile and worn inspires him to seek out the same feeling in digital forms, often imparting dramatic textures and restricted color palettes into his work.

Continually striving to bring the familiarity of the physical to the limitless options of digital art, Jordan is routinely evolving meshed techniques to create something new. He is always looking for the next project to bring to life through these means.
